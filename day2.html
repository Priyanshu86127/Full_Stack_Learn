About college

Government Engineering College Vaishali

Government Engineering College (GEC) Vaishali is a technical institution in Bihar that offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programs.
It was established in 2018 by the Government of Bihar.

Features:


GEC Vaishali is known for its innovative and skill-based education.
It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in a variety of engineering disciplines.
The college is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University (BEU) in Patna.
It is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE).
GEC Vaishali has a placement cell that helps students find jobs.
