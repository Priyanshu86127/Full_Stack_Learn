About Vaishali

Vaishali, located in the state of Bihar, is an important historical and cultural district.
It is known as the birthplace of Lord Mahavira and also holds significance in Buddhist history.

Historical Significance

Vaishali was once the capital of the Licchavi Republic, considered to be one of the first republics in the world.
It also hosted the last sermon of Lord Buddha.

  • Ancient capital of the Licchavi kingdom
  • Birthplace of Lord Mahavira
  • Buddha give his last sermon here

Tourist

Some of the famous tourist destinations in Vaishali include:

  • Vishwa Santi Stupa
  • Ashoka Piller
  • Relic Stupa
  • Museum
